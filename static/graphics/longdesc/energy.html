Overview

This chart shows the energy generation by source in selected European countries. For each country, the percentage of generated energy according to its source from 2002 to 2018 is shown. Energy sources may be one of the following: renewable, nuclear, coal, and other fossil fuels.

Presentation

This chart is made up of a number of individual area charts, one per country. Each area chart is divided into energy sources on the y-axis and individual years on the x-axis such that the total area for each year adds up to 100%.
