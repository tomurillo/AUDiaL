Overview

This bar chart shows the population of Austria divided into states, years, and sex. For each Austrian state, the population for the years 1994, 2004, and 2014 is shown in a stacked bar. Each stacked bar is further divided into two metric bars, namely, the male and female population for a given state and year is displayed.

Presentation

This bar chart is made up of several grouped stacked bars. Each group of bars corresponds to one Austrian state and consists of a line-up of three bars, one per year (1994, 2004, and 2014). Each stacked bar is divided into a blue bar which corresponds to the male population, and a red bar displaying the female population for its corresponding state and year. The metric axis to the left of the bars is labelled from 0 to 1926000, indicating number of people.
